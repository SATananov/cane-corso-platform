# Step 35 — USG Breed Standard Exact Proportions Pack

Status: ready for local browser/typecheck verification.

## Scope

Step 35 adds a public educational breed-standard layer with exact Cane Corso proportions, USG black/gold technical diagram cards, source discipline, owner photo guidance, and Knowledge article integration.

## Exact ratios preserved

- Body length = height at withers + 11%.
- Head length = 36% of height at withers.
- Muzzle : skull ≈ 1 : 2.
- Muzzle width = muzzle length; nasal bridge straight.
- Skull and muzzle upper longitudinal axes are slightly convergent.
- Skull width at zygomatic arches is equal to or greater than skull length depending on source wording.

## Source discipline

Primary source: FCI Standard N°343 — Cane Corso Italiano.
Support / cross-check: AKC official standard, UKC Cane Corso Italiano breed standard, CCAA Judge’s Manual for educational context only.

## Safety boundary

USG diagrams are educational visualizations based on official standards. They are not official FCI diagrams, not automatic judging, and not a certificate promise.
