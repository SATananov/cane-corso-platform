USG Community Rating Foundation

Included in this patch:
- Community rating foundation for Registry and Partners
- 1 member account = 1 rating (1-5), updatable later
- Self-rating blocked for own Cane Corso / own partner profile
- Community rating stays separate from:
  - admin approval
  - publication state
  - USG certificate
- Public profile pages now show a community rating panel
- Public cards now show compact community summary when ratings exist
- Guest users are guided to member access before rating
- Success/error feedback is returned on the same page after rating attempts
